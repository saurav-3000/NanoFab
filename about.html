About 3D PrintHub

Revolutionizing 3D printing one model at a time

Our Story

NanoFab is an upcoming 3D printing service based in Nepal, dedicated to making high-quality, custom 3D printing accessible to everyone. Founded by Saurav, an engineering student and CAD designer, our goal is to provide reliable, precision-crafted 3D-printed products for makers, businesses, and professionals.

As we prepare for our official launch, we're working hard to build a platform where you can bring your ideas to life-whether you need prototypes, custom parts, or artistic creations. Stay tuned as we finalize our services and get ready to serve the growing 3D printing community in Nepal!

Our Mission & Values

At 3D PrintHub, we're committed to empowering creativity through accessible, high-quality 3D printable designs. We believe that 3D printing has the power to transform how we create, customize, and consume products.

Quality First

Every model on our platform is rigorously tested to ensure perfect printability and exceptional quality.

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible with 3D printing, exploring new techniques and applications.

Community

We foster a supportive community where makers can share knowledge, inspiration, and feedback.

Sustainability

We promote responsible 3D printing practices and sustainable materials to minimize environmental impact.
