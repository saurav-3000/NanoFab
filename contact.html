Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased models?

After purchasing a model, you can download it from your account dashboard under the "My Purchases" section. Each model comes with detailed printing instructions and is available in multiple file formats.

What file formats do your 3D models come in?

All our models come in STL format, which is compatible with most 3D printers and slicing software. Many models also include additional formats like OBJ and 3MF for more advanced users.

Do you offer refunds if a model doesn't print correctly?

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our models. If you experience issues printing a model due to design flaws, we offer a full refund or replacement. Please contact our support team with details of the issue.

Can I sell items I make with your 3D models?

Our standard license allows you to print and sell physical items made with our models, as long as you don't redistribute the digital files themselves. Some models may have specific licensing terms, so please check the product details page.

How do I become a designer on your platform?

We're always looking for talented designers to join our marketplace. Please visit our "Become a Designer" page to submit your portfolio and application. We review all applications and will contact you if your work meets our quality standards.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we offer custom 3D modeling services for special projects. Please contact us with details about your project requirements, timeline, and budget, and our design team will get back to you with a quote.
